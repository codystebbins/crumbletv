Broadcast your kitchen to the world.

Live cooking for everyone.

Help Us Out

We are a team of student designers and developers and we are creating a website that allows anyone to broadcast a livestream to the world. The concept is relativly simple, our site revolves around cooking and allowing others to view a stream and cook along with the chef/entertainer. Since this is a project that has a fairly specific audience, a bit of research in understanding the community is required. We would love to hear ideas on what features you would want to see in a website that specifically targets your community (ie. you would want to search by dietary restrictions/ingredients/etc). We have prepared an initial survey.

In addition, if you want to follow the project, which is set to launch in about 4-5 months, you can sign up to our mailing list to keep updated with the project.

Crumble is a student run project out the the University of Washington's Informatics program.
